1 Information We Collect

1.1 Account and User Information

When you create an account, access the application, or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, company name, designation or role
  • Contact details and login / authentication information
  • User role, permissions, and access level within the platform

1.2 Project and Commercial Information

Users may upload, create, or process project-related information within the platform, including:

  • Project names, descriptions, estimates, and cost breakdowns
  • BOQ items, resource data, and budget information
  • Cost-control records and vendor or subcontractor rates
  • Labour, material, equipment, and productivity data
  • Attachments, documents, spreadsheets, or other project files

Such data remains the responsibility of the user or subscribing organisation.

1.3 Technical and Usage Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including IP address, browser type, device and operating system, pages visited, login activity, usage and error logs, and date and time of access. This information is used for security, performance monitoring, troubleshooting, and improvement of the platform.

1.4 Communication Information

When you contact us by email, website form, or other communication channels, we may retain that information for support, enquiry handling, and business correspondence.

2 How We Use Information

We may use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to Costimo.ai services and manage user accounts
  • To operate estimate, budget, and cost-control functions
  • To process, store, and retrieve user-created project data
  • To provide customer support and improve platform performance
  • To maintain security and prevent unauthorised access
  • To troubleshoot errors and communicate important updates
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations
  • To protect our rights, platform, users, and business interests

We do not sell user data.

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the necessary authority to upload, enter, process, or share any project, commercial, vendor, contractual, or third-party information through the platform.

Costimo.ai does not claim ownership over project data entered or uploaded by users.
